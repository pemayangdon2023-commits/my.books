My interest in books

I have always been very interested in reading books. From the moment I discovered stories, I enjoyed exploring different worlds and learning about new things. Reading is not just a hobby for me; it is something that makes me happy and excited. I like reading books of many kinds, such as stories, novels, and even short articles. Each book teaches me something new and makes me curious to know more.

Reading books has helped me improve my English a lot. When I read, I learn new words and understand how sentences are formed. It also helps me know how to express my thoughts better. Sometimes, I read stories more than once to understand them clearly, and this makes my vocabulary stronger. Reading regularly has made me more confident in speaking and writing English.

Books have also taught me important lessons about life and people. Through reading, I can understand different feelings, ideas, and experiences. It is like traveling without leaving home. The more I read, the more I feel that my imagination grows, and my mind becomes sharper. Reading has truly been a big part of my life, and I want to continue learning through books every day.
